# Ethan Smith — Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) Playbook

**Source:** Lenny's Podcast — CEO, Graphite (18 years in SEO)

Ethan ranks this the **second-biggest shift in SEO history** (after Panda killed programmatic spam). He separates what works in the ChatGPT/Perplexity era from what no longer does.

## AEO = LLM + RAG, Not Training Data

- Influencing the **core model** is borderline impossible and takes a year to show.
- All the leverage is on the **RAG layer**: what the model retrieves and cites at query time.
- You don't need to be in training data; you need to be in tomorrow's citation set.

## Head: Citation Frequency Beats Rank

- In Google: top blue link wins.
- In ChatGPT: LLM summarizes **many citations** — the product mentioned **most times** wins.
- Ranking #1 in citations isn't enough if you're mentioned fewer times than competitors.
- Implication: early-stage startups can win AEO in days (via one Reddit thread, YouTube video, or blog) whereas SEO typically requires Series A+ domain authority.

## Tail: Chat Queries Are Longer

- Average Perplexity query ~**25 words** vs. Google's ~6.
- Massive long-tail of never-before-asked follow-up questions.
- Strategy: mine Reddit, customer support, and sales call questions — those are the prompts people are asking.
- New long-tail SEO is back, just inside chat.

## The Three Citation Tiers

- **Onsite:** traditional SEO + answer every follow-up question thoroughly.
- **Offsite UGC:** Reddit, Quora, YouTube/Vimeo (heavily weighted by ChatGPT's trust-tuned retrieval).
- **Offsite affiliates:** Dotdash Meredith, Good Housekeeping, Investopedia — "the most successful SEO company of all time" and the most-cited in LLMs.

## Reddit: The Anti-Spam Layer

- Spam farms (100 fake accounts, upvote rings) get detected and banned — Reddit's community polices itself.
- Working strategy at Webflow: real employees post with their name + role + a useful answer. Five comments beats 10,000 fake ones.
- Deel grew this way pre-AI; same pattern now works for AEO.

## The Win: LLM Traffic Converts 6x

- **Webflow saw a 6x conversion-rate delta** between LLM traffic and Google Search traffic.
- Why: multi-turn prompts pre-qualify; by click, intent is fully formed.
- LLM traffic uplift at Graphite's clients started ~**January 2025** — coincided with clickable cards/maps in answers plus adoption.

## Content Quality Heuristic

- Most content is "non-experts rewriting each other's articles" chasing content-scoring tools.
- Good content = **information gain + atypicality + domain expertise + original research**.
- 1 in 20 landing pages drives ~85% of traffic — concentrate spend on the few pages that will actually rank.

## Key Takeaways

- AEO is a RAG problem, not a training problem — optimize citations, not the model.
- Get mentioned in many citations; being ranked #1 isn't enough.
- Reddit + YouTube + top affiliates (Dotdash) are the highest-leverage offsite surfaces.
- Long-tail is back — answer the 25-word follow-up questions nobody has answered.
- LLM traffic converts up to 6x better than Google search traffic.
- Write for information gain; rewriting competitors' articles is a dead channel.
