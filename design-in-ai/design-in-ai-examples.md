# Examples and Resources

Pointers to real-world Design.md files, curated collections, tutorials and research.

## Curated Collections

### VoltAgent awesome-design-md
[github.com/VoltAgent/awesome-design-md](https://github.com/VoltAgent/awesome-design-md)

66+ Design.md files extracted from public websites (light and dark variants). Brands include:
- **AI Platforms:** Claude, Cohere, ElevenLabs, Mistral AI, Ollama
- **Design/Dev Tools:** Figma, Framer, Miro, Webflow, Vercel, Supabase
- **Fintech:** Stripe, Wise, Binance, Coinbase, Revolut
- **Technology:** Apple, Spotify, Uber, IBM, NVIDIA
- **Other:** Tesla, Ferrari, BMW, Notion, Linear, Raycast

### TypeUI Registry
[typeui.sh](https://www.typeui.sh/) — 50+ community-contributed design skill files. Browse by style, download and customise.

### Agent Skills Registry
[agentskills.so](https://agentskills.so/) — Central registry where Design.md files are listed as shareable AI agent skills.

## Key Takeaways

- VoltAgent (66+ brands) and TypeUI (50+ skills) are the two main starting-point repositories
- Real-world adoption evidence is primarily from startups and individual developers, not enterprise
- Uber is the main enterprise case study for AI design documentation at scale
- Academic research (Columbia DAPLab, ArXiv) confirms that structured, contextual documentation outperforms traditional README-style docs for AI agents
